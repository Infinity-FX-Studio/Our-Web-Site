About Us

INFINITY FX is a pioneering multi-disciplinary new media art studio working with emerging technology to create magical artistic experiences.

We design experiences born from our ethereal imaginations to influence surroundings and receive compelling feedback and rewards in the form of sensory developments. The aim is to amaze the audience by inviting them to become a part of a poetic, surreal, colorful and powerful digital environment.

Being a pioneer in Anamorphic Illusion 3D art installations in Turkey, our expertise stretches from public art, event design with live audio, visuals, light shows by interactive art content. By creating immersive art we are offering visitors a unique experience which is achieved by the image and the content displayed over 360° in video projection, giving the impression of being within the projected work. This is a new and exciting form of projection mapping, where rather than just giving spectacular imagery, those images transport you to new and wonderful places and also into multi-sensory experiences using versatile combinations of 3D projection mapping, audio, haze and LED screens to create captivating visuals for large-scale events. Infinity is an expert in offering end to end production services while also providing consultancy for conceptual design and production for multi conceptual projects.

Our passionate team values creativity, joy, speed, curiosity, an appetite for success, and eagerness to learn.
